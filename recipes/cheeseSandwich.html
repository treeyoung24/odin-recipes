Avocado Toast Recipe

About the recipe

This is a recipe about Cheese Sandwich, a very delicous and simple meal that you can make within the comfort of your kitchen

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Nuts (Optional)
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Put the Cheese evenly onto the bread
    3. \
  3. Microwave for 5 minutes or until the cheese start to melt
  4. sprinkle salt, pepper or nuts if needed
