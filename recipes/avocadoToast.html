Avocado Toast Recipe

About the recipe

This is a recipe about avocado toast, a very healthy and simple breakfast that you can make within the comfort of your kitchen

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Bread
  • Nuts (Optional)
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Spread the avocado evenly onto the bread
  3. sprinkle salt, pepper or nuts if needed
